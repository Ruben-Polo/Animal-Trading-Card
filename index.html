Caracal

The Caracal is a feline that measures between 60-90cm and has a peculiar long ears

  • Scientific Name: Caracal caracal
  • Average Length: 60-90 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 17-19 years
  • Habitat: Africa and Asia

The Caracal is a feline that lives in the deserts of Africa and Asia. They have the ability to jump vertically up to 3 meters, which is why their favorite prey are birds. They are solitary animals that have a life expectancy in captivity of about 17 years.
